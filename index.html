What is it?

It is the prototype of a game based on gravitation.

You are the orange thing, imagine that it is a space ship and that you are the commander.

The black thing in the center is a Black Hole. Currently, it does not matter if you fall into it, but actually if you 'touch' the Black Hole then you are dead.
